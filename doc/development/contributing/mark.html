Mark Use¶

Name¶

Digital Rebar usage options are as follows:

  1. The project name is “Digital Rebar” as two words, both capitalized
  2. Acceptable alternatives:
digital_rebar digitalRebar rebar.digital dR Internally within the project, it is acceptable to just say “Rebar”

Mascot¶

Dr. Mo is the Digital Rebar mascot.

It is accepable to: 1. add clothing and accoutrements 1. change Dr. Mo’s expression

Dr. Mo has no gender and should not be referenced with gender specific pronouns (he or she).
